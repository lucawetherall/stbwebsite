The church will be open on Tuesdays from 10 am to 4 pm and on Sundays from 2 to 5 pm for private prayer.

Proper provision has been made for the safety of all visitors including  demarcated pews, the provision of hand sanitiser and separate entry and exit points which have been clearly signposted.

It is hoped that access to this beautiful sacred space is an opportunity for comfort and refreshment for as many people as possible.  As a parish, we continue to explore [ways of serving each other and our neighbour](https://landing.churchdesk.com/b/59140/listening-organising-and-responding) in the name of Christ during this second lockdown and beyond.
