**We have a variety of regular activities and events at St Barnabas. Here are some of our main events running during the month of June:**

* * *

**Ealing Gospel Choir | 10 June & 17 June, 3 - 5pm**

**<img src="/images/news/a80d4e99-o_2F1901_2Fscreen.shot.2018-05-03.at.11.31.33.webp" alt="Regular Events & Activities | June 2018" loading="lazy" decoding="async" width="370" height="208">**

Join the Gospel Choir every second and third Sunday of the month for a great time of energetic singing. No experience necessary! There is a small charge of £2 per person for rehearsal. All ages welcome from 8 years old upwards. 

* * *

**Thursday Pop-in | Every Thursday, 2.30pm**  

<img src="/images/news/1814bfe2-coffee-cup-mug-drink.jpg" alt="Regular Events & Activities | June 2018" loading="lazy" decoding="async" width="370" height="277">

  

Come along for a cup of tea and a chat in the large hall on Thursday afternoons. Everyone is welcome!

* * *

**Friday Lunchtime Concerts | Every Friday, 1pm**   

All concerts are on Friday at 1pm and last for about 45 minutes.   The concerts are free with a voluntary retiring collection.

<img src="/images/news/55bc0394-black-and-white-black-and-white-close-up-586415.jpg" alt="Regular Events & Activities | June 2018" loading="lazy" decoding="async" width="370" height="278">

In June, our concerts are:

**June 1st  Ke Ma (piano)**  

Schubert: Piano Sonata in G major D 894  
Schubert-Liszt: Two Song Transcriptions  
  
**June 8th The English Piano Trio**  
Haydn: Piano trio in G minor Hob XV:19  
Beethoven: Piano trio in D Op 70 no 1 ‘Ghost’

**June 15th  Michal Szymanowski (piano)**  
Grieg: Ballade in G minor Op 24  
Chopin: Ballade in F minor Op 52  
Chopin: Barcarolle in F sharp major Op 60  
Scriabin: Sonata no 4 in F sharp major Op 30

**June 22nd  The Blaze Ensemble**  
Wagner: Siegfried Idyll  
Spohr: Nonet in F Op 31

**June 29th Nigel Blomiley (cello) Siu Chui Li (piano)**  
Mozart: Violin sonata in E minor K304 (transcribed for cello)  
Brahms: Violin sonata in G Op 78 (transcribed for cello)  
Schumann: Fantasiestucke Op 73

Please refer to the website for further dates and information:

**Pitshanger Pictures |**   

Pitshanger Pictures exists to inform, entertain and strengthen community in North Ealing by screening films that stimulate conversation and discussion on questions in life.

<img src="/images/news/31dceabb-screen.shot.2018-04-24.at.14.36.51.webp" alt="Regular Events & Activities | June 2018" loading="lazy" decoding="async" width="370" height="239">

No need to book tickets, just come along. Doors open half an hour before the screenings unless otherwise specified.

**Wednesdays – tickets £5 Saturdays & other screenings – child (under 16) £6, adult £8 (includes drink and snack) Pitshanger Pictures Opera – £10 (additional cost for food).**

See our website to find out what films are showing during June on

[https://www.barnabites.org/pag...](https://www.barnabites.org/page/15/pitshanger-pictures)
