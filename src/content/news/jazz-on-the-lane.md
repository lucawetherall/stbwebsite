Join us for our annual Jazz on the Lane on 10th June!

  

10.30 | A Lively Jazz Mass

An exciting mass to celebrate the patronal festival of St Barnabas. The band will be playing the Bob Chilcott Mass setting making it a joyful services with great energy and swing! Come along and watch the band and choir play and sing from the heart.

  

13.00-1800 | Jazz in the Garden

We have a fantastic line up of local jazz bands and artists, with our headline act, Rum Buffalo - A band who have toured th eUK and around the world with their unique sound of deep hip-hop rhythms coated in bass synths and infectious horns.

  

ADMISSION IS FREE.

  

There will also be food and drink available for purchase including a Hog Roast, salad lunch and bar. Plus, family-friendly entertainment with children's entertainer.

  

Come and join the fun!
