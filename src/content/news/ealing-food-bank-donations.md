Each Harvest festival, we invite you to bring offerings of food and household products to donate to the Ealing Food Bank. They do a wonderful job helping those in our community who are in need, providing support and nutritionally balanced emergency food. Please help us support them by donating products listed below.   
  

-   BREAKFAST CEREAL
-   UHT MILK - WHOLE MILK, 1 LITRE
-   TINNED MEAT & FISH
-   LONG LIFE SPONGE PUDDINGS
-   POTATOES - TINNED AND INSTANT
-   LONG LIFE FRUIT JUICE AND SQUASH
-   KETCHUP & MAYO
-   NAPPIES - SIZE 6 ONLY (SMALL PACKS)
-   SHOWER GEL & HAIR SHAMPOO
-   SHAVING FOAM AND RAZORS
