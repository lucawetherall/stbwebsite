**A Message from Amy Merriman** 

‘Hi everyone: my name’s Amy. I’ve been a part of the Ministry Team here at St Barnabas for over a year, now, and am delighted that I can continue to serve in the parish as I train for the ordained ministry. Thank you to everyone who has helped me feel at home so quickly, and for your ongoing prayers as I’ve been discerning a call. A bit about me: I’m originally from Swansea in South Wales. My work background has been a combination of lay ministry and higher education: I currently work at King’s College London as a member of the Dean’s Office and Chaplaincy team - I’m Programme Manager for a theology/philosophy/ethics course we run (which I enjoy hugely!). I look forward to our ongoing journey together.’

_Amy Merriman_
