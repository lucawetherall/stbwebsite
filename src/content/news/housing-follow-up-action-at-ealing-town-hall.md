Tuesday 22 May, 6.30pm

After our very successful Ealing Housing Assembly in April, we now need to ensure the council delivers.  Join us and other Ealing Citizens as we gather outside Ealing Town Hall to welcome the council leader and new cabinet. We’ll be handing out pledge cards to councillors as a reminder of commitments made at the Housing Assembly including measures to combat rogue landlords, attaining 50% genuinely affordable housing in all new developments and identifying a site for Ealing’s first Community Land Trust within 6 months. Please let a member of the clergy, Paul Robinson or JJ Jung know you can attend on Tuesday after today’s Mass.
