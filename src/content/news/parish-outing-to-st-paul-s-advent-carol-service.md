Unfortunately we will not be holding an Advent Carol Service this year. However, we are making a parish outing to the **Advent Carol Service at St Paul’s Cathedral** **on Saturday the 27th of November, 5:30pm.** This will be a truly moving experience: 

_‘One of the year’s most dramatic services sung by the Choir of St Paul’s Cathedral. A candlelit procession moves from darkness to light as words and music help us focus on the Advent themes of light in darkness, hope in despair and a new relationship with God in Christ.’  – [St Paul’s Cathedral](https://www.stpauls.co.uk/christmas-2021)_  

* * *

**Opportunity for children and young people**  

The Cathedral are also inviting young people from across the Diocese to be part of the procession of light which moves through the Cathedral as part of the processions. There is a rehearsal earlier in the afternoon for this, when the children and supervising adults will be taken through the procession.

* * *

If you would like to attend and/or if your child would like to participate in the procession, [please email Fr Justin](mailto:%20vicar@barnabites.net) by **no later than Friday 19th November, 2021**. We need to inform the Cathedral of numbers in order to obtain our free tickets.
