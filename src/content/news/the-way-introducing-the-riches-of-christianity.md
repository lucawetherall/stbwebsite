'Would you describe yourself as spiritual but not religious?' 

'Do you have questions (or niggling reservations) about religion, spirituality and the big philosophical issues?'

_Sunday 24 June to Sunday 22 July_  

‘The Way’ is an opportunity to explore the beauty of the Christian tradition in an setting that encourages curiosity and debate without any expectations or obligations. Some of the themes explored in the 6 session course will include contemplation and prayer, the meaning and power of community, the ancient practices of Christians and the mystic/revolutionary/teacher called Jesus Christ who claimed to be God.

**How does it work?**

The course will run on Sunday afternoons between 12:30 pm and 14:30 pm (except for Quiet Morning - see below). It begins with a simple sandwich lunch and will include some input followed by discussion and reflection around.a specific theme. 

It will consist of 6 sessions:

**Sunday 24 June**

**Sunday 1 July**

**(Saturday 7 July - Quiet morning, 10 am to 2 pm)**

**Sunday 8 July**

**Sunday 15 July**

**Sunday 22 July** 

**Do I need to sign up?**

You are welcome simply to arrive at the first session but it is always helpful for us to know how many will be attending. An **online attendance form** can be found at:

None at all. We are delighted to have you as our guest.
