**Regular Services in July:**

**8am Low Mass** | This is a reflective service using the beautiful language of the 1662 Book of Common Prayer lasting approximately 45 minutes.

**10.30am Sung High Mass** | Our main service of the day combines transcendent music, beautiful ceremonial and accessible preaching. It lasts approximately 1 hour 15 minutes and includes ministry for children, from toddlers to teens. 

**Wednesday Mid-week Mass |** at 12.30pm in the Lady Chapel 

A reflective communion service and short sermon for midweek spiritual refreshment.

**Special Services in July:**

**’S**hh\-Free’ Mass | 10.30am, 7th July

This is truly a ’Shh-free’ form of Eucharistic worship that is open to all ages, but particularly suitable for families. Music is led by the Ealing Gospel Choir.

Children’s Breakfast Mass** | 9.30am, 21st July
