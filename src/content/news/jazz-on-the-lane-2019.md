Join us for a fantastic day, in celebration of the patronal festival of St Barnabas, at Ealing’s favourite summer event!  
  
10.30am JAZZ MASS  
  
A lively Jazz Mass to celebrate St Barnabas day led by a jazz band and extended choir. It’s a very energetic service that is suitable for ALL AGES!  
  
1pm to 6pm JAZZ IN THE GARDEN  
  
Live Jazz returns to Pitshanger Lane with our headline act, Sound of 17 and a great line up of local musicians. As always, we have a whole range of refreshments including a delicious hog roast, salad lunch, bar and Hook & Cleaver burgers for purchase throughout the afternoon.  
  
There is also an entertainer for little ones, so mum and dad can have the afternoon to enjoy and relax too!  
  
Free admission!
