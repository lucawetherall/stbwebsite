Dear Friends, 

It is with genuine sadness that I announce my departure from St Barnabas in the forthcoming weeks. Your warm and welcoming nature has profoundly shaped my experience here, enriching my future ministry in immeasurable ways. The valuable insights and lessons I have gathered from each of you have made my placement here so worthwhile. Although I say goodbye, I will be back soon to say hello.

Where am I going I hear you ask?! I’ll be ordained Deacon into the Parish of Bulkington in the Diocese of Coventry, at Coventry Cathedral on Sunday 2nd July at 4pm. The dean of the cathedral assures us there’s lots of space for non ticket holders, so if any of you are in the area, feel free to come along. Your prayers though, would be warmly welcomed as I move and prepare for the day.

With love, 

Robin
