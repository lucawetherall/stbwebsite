**Sublime Sacred Sounds  - Incarnation**

In ‘The Journey to Bethlehem’ we explore four themes relating to Advent and Christmas: Prophecy, the Forerunner, Annunciation, and Incarnation. In this sequence of sacred word and music we explore ‘incarnation’ in the overall theme of Advent.

[**Sign up** to get a weekly reminder.](https://forms.churchdesk.com/f/AnfVjCnA-J) 

Sublime Sacred Sounds is a weekly dose of sacred word and music. Presented online by the musicians and clergy of St Barnabas Ealing, each weekly episode explores a specific theme weaving together transcendent music and moving spoken word.
