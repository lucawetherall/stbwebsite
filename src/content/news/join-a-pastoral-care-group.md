**There are a number of pastoral care groups that connect parishioners to one another broadly within their neighbourhoods.** 

The purpose of each group is to be mutually supportive through prayer and undertaking simple acts of kindness. 

If you aren't already in a group we would be delighted to help you join one. Please email [Felicity Mather](mailto:felicity@hughmather.uk).
