A new C.A.P ([Christians Against Poverty](https://capuk.org/)) Debt Centre, based in Oaktree Fellowship, Acton, is in need of volunteers who can provide ongoing support. 

They are currently looking for people to volunteer for the following roles:

-   Prayer Team
    -   People who have a heart to pray for the needs of our clients and the various aspects related to the smooth running of the Centre. 
-   Befrienders 
    -   People who can be available to meet clients with myself and show love and compassion.
-   Blessings Team
    -   People who can help with writing cards, organise small Christmas gifts and occasional hospitality.

_The most urgent role we are seeking to cover is for Befrienders as we are already seeing a large number of clients due to the current financial climate._
