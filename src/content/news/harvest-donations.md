The Food Pantry at St Barnabas Church has been working really well, receiving and donating food and other essentials for those who are struggling to provide for their families in these difficult times. Thank you so much for all you have done so far. 

**Most Needed Items**

Rice, pasta, flour, pulses  tinned, beans, tomatoes, spaghetti, soup, vegetables, fruit, meat, fish, jam, marmalade, peanut butter, marmite, breakfast cereal, long-life milk (all types), bicuits, tea, coffee, condiments  (ketchup, brown sauce, mayonnaise, etc.), oils, cook-in-sauces, squash, long- life fruit juice, toiletries, cleaning products, washing powder/liquid nappies (unopened packs), toilet paper. 

Harvest Festival will be celebrated at St Barnabas on  Sunday 18th October (10.30am) and Tuesday 20 October (1 pm) where gifts will be received and blessed. 

**From 28th October the Pantry will be open on WEDNESDAYS from 1pm-4pm (also Saturdays 12-3pm).**
