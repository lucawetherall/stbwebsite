Dear All,

This Saturday is our Come & Sing Vivaldi Gloria led by our Director of Music Kavi Pau. It's a fantastic opportunity to sing with St Barnabas Choir as well as singers from Ensemble 1517. If you enjoy singing or have always wanted to try singing a choir, come and join in the fun! Rehearsals start at 1pm with a concert at 6pm. 

If you just want to listen and enjoy the music, please do come along for the concert!

Singers: £10 including score hire and refreshments. 

Audience: £10 on the door. 

To book your place email **music@barnabites.org**.
