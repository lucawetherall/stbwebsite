Bangers & Sparklers| Saturday 3rd November, 5.30-7pm  
  
The perfect bonfire night for the smallest ones among us. Come along for a wonderful time at our Bangers and Sparklers! A 'bang-free' evening with a giant bonfire, sparklers, hot dogs and marshmallow roasting! It's a great evening for all the family.  
  
Tickets can be purchased online at: [https://pay.churchdesk.com/SyMNmZWqQ](https://pay.churchdesk.com/SyMNmZWqQ)  
Adults (£5) includes mulled wine and a banger roll  
Children (£2.50) includes a drink, banger roll and a pack of sparklers!  
  
Please help us to spread the word with other mums, dads, grandparents and carers by RSVP'ing to our [Facebook event](http://www.facebook.com/events/479616822545149/) and sharing!
