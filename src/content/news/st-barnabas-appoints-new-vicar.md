New Vicar announced for St Barnabas!

**Revd Sarah Howard-Jones**

We are delighted to announce that Sarah has been appointed as the next Vicar of St Barnabas and we look forward to welcoming her later this year. Sarah is currently serving as Curate at St Martin’s, Kensal Rise.
