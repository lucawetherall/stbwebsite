Godparents’ Sunday 

On Sunday 6th May at 10.30am, we will be celebrating Godparents’ Day here at St Barnabas along with many other churches around the UK. 

This is a great opportunity for families to invite Godparents to come along and worship with their godchildren, and to involve all those in the congregation who are godparents or godchildren too. Godparents are really significant family members. So please start thinking about how you might involve your godparents on this special day. Invitation cards and posters will be available nearer the time.
