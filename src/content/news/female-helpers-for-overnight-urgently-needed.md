The Night Shelter is well under way, offering a warm and safe haven to our homeless guests during this cold period. However, we are looking for some ladies who might do an overnight shift or two in the Shelter. Four volunteers are required (two female and two male) for each night shift at the shelter and the shift times are 9.45pm/10pm to 6.30am. However, it is not necessary to stay awake all night! Only one male and one female volunteer needs to be awake at any one time so the shift is divided into two so each volunteer is only awake for half of the night. Comfy camp beds are provided to sleep on so you only need a sleeping bag!

**The remaining dates are:**

**4/5 March;**

**11/12 March;**

**18/19 March;**

**25/26 March.**

If you can help, please let Dn Jill know.
