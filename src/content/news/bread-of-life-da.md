A reflection on the last supper, celebrate the meal of bread and wine with Jesus, the ever present King, through the medium of dance performance. It is a stirring depiction of how God is building a community of love with Jesus himself at the centre. The performance by Springs Dance Company is a vibrant and contemporary dance organisation bringing creative and engaging dance to a diverse range of venues and communities. 

They will be performing for us on Palm Sunday, 25th March at 7.30pm at St Barnabas Church. 

The performance runs for approximately 50 mins. 

Tickets are available from the Parish office will be purchasable online soon.
