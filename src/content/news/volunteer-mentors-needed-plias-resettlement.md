[Plias Resettlement](https://www.pliasresettlement.co.uk/) is looking to recruit volunteer mentors for their young people. They are a charity that supports offenders’ rehabilitation.

  

_Mentors will possess the following:_ 

Characteristics

Reliable, Committed, Flexible, Respectful, Empathic, Sense of Humour, Patient, Open, Sensitive, Non-judgmental, Self-aware, Tactful

Knowledge  

Life Experience, Issues facing Ex-offenders, Young people, CJS Procedures, Boundaries and Limitations, Local knowledge

Skills

Active Listening, Interpersonal Engagement, Time Keeping, Problem Solving, Motivating, Communication Skills, Reflective, Self-learner, Leadership

_The volunteering commitment will include the following:_

-   A minimum 6-month commitment
-   2 hours a week (face to face, telephone or online, subject to agreement) including 1 hour of mentoring and 1 hour to write up notes and carry out any research to support the mentoring sessions
-   A basic DBS check will be carried out
-   Mentor training will be provided
-   Optional further training available

If interested or seeking more information, please contact Georgiana Bor at georgiana.bor@plias.co.uk.
