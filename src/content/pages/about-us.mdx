We are the people of west London — diverse in ages, ethnicities, sexual orientation and
relationship status. We are a sacramental people, encountering God in the challenging but
joyful work of making communion together. And we are a hopeful people, working to build
liveable communities through a culture of encouragement and practical action.

We believe the Gospel offers an unconditional and life-giving invitation to the banquet of
God’s creation, as well as a call to protect it. We strive to offer holy service to our
neighbours and to those on the margins.

We are inclusive in our welcome, our worship, and in celebrating and affirming the lives of
all — whatever your economic power, gender, sexuality, disability, ethnicity or mental
health. We are a member of [Inclusive Church](https://www.inclusive-church.org/).
