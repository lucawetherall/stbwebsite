Our Food Pantry is about much more than handing out food. The focus is on building
relationships that enhance the dignity of everyone involved. Sharing food with our neighbours
is at the heart of how we understand Christian community life.

Volunteers build ongoing relationships with guests, providing both immediate weekly
provisions and longer-life products according to need. Everyone is welcome — there is no
referral required.

## How you can help

- **Donate food** — items can be brought after Sunday services or during Wednesday pantry
  hours.
- **Give** — you can support the Pantry’s work with a
  [one-off donation](https://donate.churchdesk.com/en-gb/1/CFopNzPKS).
- **Volunteer** — to join the team, please [contact the parish office](/contact-us).

**Open:** Wednesdays, 1–3pm, in the church halls.
