The **Being With** course emphasises community, presence and attention as pathways to
understanding our spirituality. It offers an inclusive, sacramental and positive approach to
Christianity — well suited to those who are new to faith, those who want to deepen it, and
those returning after time away from church.

There are no silly questions, and no pressure. Whatever you believe, or aren’t sure you
believe, you are welcome.

The course runs from time to time through the year. To find out when the next one begins,
please [contact the clergy](/contact-us) or keep an eye on our weekly newsletter.
