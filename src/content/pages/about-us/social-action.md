Christianity is much more than a set of religious beliefs; it is a way of life. Service is
central to Christian practice — we are called not to be served but to serve — and it is in
self-giving that we find spiritual fulfilment and unity with Christ.

Our social action focuses on three things:

- **Caring for one another** within the church community.
- **Serving our local community** through Christ-centred hospitality.
- **Engaging as active, informed citizens**, locally and globally.

## How we serve

- [Winter Night Shelter](/about-us/social-action/winter-night-shelter) — hosting and
  volunteering with the Ealing Churches Winter Night Shelter
- [Food Pantry](/community/food-pantry-at-st-barnabas) — open to all, every Wednesday
- [Pastoral Care](/about-us/pastoral-care) — walking alongside people in every situation
