---

There is step-free access through the main entrance, which has wide doors. The halls and the
Lady Chapel are step-free, with a permanent ramp into the Lady Chapel.

Welcomers assist throughout our services and can bring communion to anyone who would prefer
to stay seated; a first aider is usually present. Wheelchairs are available to borrow, and we
have one designated accessibility toilet equipped for mobility needs.

An induction loop operates in the church, and assistance dogs are very welcome.

We are always trying to do better. If there is something that would make your visit easier,
please [tell us](/contact-us) — your feedback helps us improve.
