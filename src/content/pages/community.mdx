We are proud to be part of the vibrant community life of Pitshanger Lane, with a regular
programme of concerts, films and events. We also seek to listen and be attentive to the needs
of our community — to work in partnership with local groups and organisations, especially
where we share a call to be signs of hope and to walk alongside people in every situation.

## What’s going on

- **[Food Pantry](/community/food-pantry-at-st-barnabas)** — open to all, every Wednesday from
  1–3pm, no referral needed.
- **[Pitshanger Pictures](/community/pitshanger-pictures)** — our community cinema, screening
  films that spark conversation.
- **[Memory Café](/memory-cafe)** — a warm welcome every Thursday morning for those living
  with memory difficulties, and their carers.
- **Garden Team** — tends our churchyard and garden, meeting on the first Saturday morning of
  the month (with refreshments). New hands are always welcome.
- **Social events** — the Summer Fayre, the December Frost Fair and our Supper Quiz. Watch the
  posters along the Lane and our weekly newsletter for dates.
