Our Youth Group combines community service, conversation about the big questions of life and
faith, and fundraising through handmade items and snacks — all with plenty of fun and mutual
support along the way.

If you are between 10 and 16, you are very welcome. Just ask at the door on a Sunday.

**When:** 10.30am every Sunday, except during school holidays.
