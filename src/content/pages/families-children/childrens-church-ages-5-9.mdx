---

The gathering starts in the main service, and the children leave together after the opening
prayers. Each session features a fun and engaging reflection on a Bible story, a related
craft or other activity, and concludes back in church with communion or a blessing alongside
the rest of the congregation.

Anyone can join in — just ask for directions as you arrive at the main service.

**When:** Every Sunday, 10.30am, in the large hall.
