Noisy Mass with Stay & Play meets in person every Sunday at 10.30am in the small hall. Make
your way directly there, or ask the Welcomers at the main church entrance for directions.

It’s a short, lively and interactive service for the under-5s and their carers, followed by
toys and fun in the Stay & Play — a great chance for parents to chat while the little ones
play with their friends. Parents and carers who would like to receive the Eucharist are
offered the opportunity to do so at this service.

**When:** Every Sunday, 10.30am, in the small hall.
