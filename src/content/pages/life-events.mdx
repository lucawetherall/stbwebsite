Whether you are marking a beginning, making a promise, or saying goodbye, we would be glad to
walk alongside you.

## Baptisms & confirmation

Baptism (christening) is a joyful welcome into the family of the Church, for children and
adults alike. If you would like to explore baptism or confirmation, do get in touch — there is
no need to be a regular churchgoer to ask.

## Weddings

We would be honoured to host your wedding. The church is a beautiful and dignified setting,
and our clergy will guide you through every step of preparing to marry.

## Funerals

At a time of loss, we are here to help. The clergy conduct funerals for everyone, whether or
not they were churchgoers, and continue to offer bereavement support afterwards (see also our
[pastoral care](/about-us/pastoral-care)).

## How to enquire

To ask about a baptism, confirmation, wedding or funeral, please
[contact the parish office](/contact-us) or speak to a member of the clergy at any of our
services. Our clergy are also available to hear confession and to offer spiritual guidance.
