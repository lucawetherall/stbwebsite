We are passionate about supporting families in their spiritual journey and helping them
thrive within the bigger family of God’s church. Children, young people and families are
encouraged to take part in worship, spiritual growth, service and fellowship — and children
are welcome at **all** of our services.

Every Sunday at 10.30am there is something for every age:

- **[Noisy Mass](/families-children/noisy)** — ages 0–4, with Stay & Play, in the small hall.
- **[Children’s Church](/families-children/childrens-church-ages-5-9)** — ages 5–9, in the
  large hall.
- **[Youth Group](/families-children/youth-group)** — ages 10–16.

Thinking about a [baptism or christening](/life-events)? We would be glad to hear from you.
